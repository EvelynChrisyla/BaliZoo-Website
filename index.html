"Embrace the Magic of Wildlife at Bali Zoo"

Bali Zoo

Bali Zoo is a zoo located in Bali and has been open since 2002, Bali Zoo is one of the best zoos in Asia Pacific. Bali Zoo is not only used for family recreation but is also used to look after endangered animals. In Bali Zoo there are more than 600 animals

Night Safari Expedition

This Night Safari Expedition is a place that can only be visited at night. Here, you can see various attractions, go around using elephants, and can go around the zoo
